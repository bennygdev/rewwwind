Exchange and Returns

We are committed to providing you with a pleasant second-hand shopping experience at Rewwwind. We understand that sometimes purchases may not meet your expectations, and we're here to help. See the options below to get started.

Exchange Policy for Books and Records

Shop with peace of mind! You can exchange books and records purchased from our online store within 7 days of receiving your order, provided that:

  • The items are in the same condition as when delivered
  • All original packaging and labels are intact
  • You have your order confirmation email or receipt

To initiate an exchange:

  1. Contact us at support@rewwwind.com within 7 days of receiving your order
  2. Bring the items you wish to exchange to our store within 14 days of purchase
  3. You will receive store credit of equivalent value that can be used both in-store and online

Order Cancellation

Changed your mind? We understand. You can cancel your order:

  • Within 24 hours of placing your order
  • Before the order has been shipped
  • You will receive store credit of equivalent value

Please note that cancellations are not possible after the 24-hour period or once the order has been shipped.

Quality Issues

If you receive an item that:

  • Differs significantly from its description
  • Has undisclosed damage or defects
  • Is the wrong item
  • Has playback issues (for records)
  • Has missing pages or severe damage (for books)

Please contact us immediately at support@rewwwind.com with photos and details of the issue. We will work with you to resolve the situation promptly.

Returns Policy

As we deal in second-hand items, all sales are generally final. However, we understand that exceptional circumstances may arise. In such cases:

  • Returns are considered on a case-by-case basis
  • Items must be in original condition with all tags/packaging
  • Approved returns will receive store credit
  • Return shipping costs may apply unless the item was misrepresented
