Order Status

Local orders: All orders are packed and shipped within 2-7 business days on a next-day-delivery service. Business days are Monday through Friday (excluding weekends and public holidays).

International orders: All orders to Indonesia, Malaysia (West Malaysia), Hong Kong, Philippines, South Korea, Thailand, and Taiwan are packed and shipped within 2-7 business days. They may take up to 7 business days to arrive. Business days are Monday through Friday (excluding weekends and public holidays).

Some orders may experience delays due to customs. Unfortunately, we have no control over these delays and cannot predict what they may be. If necessary, we suggest that you contact your local office for additional information!

Shipping Information

Local orders: All orders above S$30 are eligible for free delivery. A shipping fee of S$4 is charged for orders below S$30.

Malaysian orders: We are delighted to offer FREE delivery for all orders to Western Malaysia. For orders to Eastern Malaysia (Sarawak, Sabah), a live quote based on prevailing shipping rates is provided by our partner, Shippit, upon checkout!

Rest of the world: We currently also ship to Australia, Hong Kong, Indonesia, Philippines, South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, United Kingdom, United States, and Vietnam.
