Trade-In FAQs

When you trade-in with us, the equivalent dollar value of your trade-in will be given to you in Vouchers. Vouchers can be spent on any Rewwwind product in our online store only.

If you can't find our email(s) / your credits, drop us an email at rewwwindhelp@gmail.com!

We use an in-house algorithm (based on current market price, popularity, market saturation and condition grade) to determine each item's trade-in value. At present, we do not offer pre-valuation.

Look out for an email* or two from us, containing your vouchers and a breakdown of the value of your items, 4 to 8 weeks after we receive your items.

You may receive your credits across different emails, as due to the nature of our operations, different item types are processed separately.

* Do check your spam/junk if you can't find our email(s)!

Of course! If you choose to donate your items, instead of trading them in for credits, we will in turn donate your credits as cash to nonprofit organisations and initiatives to support efforts in tackling urgent environmental or social issues, when they are sold.

Unfortunately, we don’t offer cash for items.
